Event

During January to March 2024, NEEV IITGN, in partnership with Desai Foundation Trust, successfully organized nine projects and activities, including skill development courses, workshops, and awareness sessions for 286 youth and women hailing from rural areas in Gandhinagar. A total of 37 participants completed the Stitching Skill Training Course, 24 participants completed the Beautician Skill Training Course, 15 participants completed the Tally Skill Training Course, 12 participants completed the Spoken English Training Course, 20 participants completed the Computer Hardware Training Course, and 25 participants completed the Training Of Trainers in Sewing, all of whom received certificates for their achievements. These courses were offered entirely free of cost to the participants. Additionally, NEEV conducted awareness sessions in Banking and Financial Literacy for 56 participants, Menstrual Health Management for 55 participants, and Career Development Skills for 43 participants.
